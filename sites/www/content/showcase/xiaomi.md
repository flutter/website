Xiaomi, a global tech leader, used Flutter to build the companion app for their popular new electric vehicle, the Xiaomi SU7. The app offers features like remote control, real-time updates, shopping, and community features, catering to both current and prospective Xiaomi car owners. Flutter's cross-platform capabilities and efficiency allowed the development team to build the app 60% faster than with native frameworks, ensuring a consistent user experience across iOS and Android. Xiaomi is excited about the results achieved with Flutter and plans to continue innovating in mobile app development.

***Our commitment to innovation, combined with the power of Flutter, will continue to drive us forward as we create groundbreaking experiences for our users.***

-Xiaomi Engineering Lead
