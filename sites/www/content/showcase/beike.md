Beike Zhaofang [NYSE: BEKE] is a leading tech driven housing service platform, and is helping 300 million families have a better experience when searching for second-hand housing, new housing, rental housing, as well as decoration, home furnishing, and other community services. Learn how Beike uses Flutter in their apps to deliver a smooth experience and fast development speed on multiple platforms, and how the team builds up their internal infrastructure with Flutter.
